I am a PhD candidate at the University of Toronto, supervised by Dimitrios Hatzinakos. I am also a Postgraduate affiliate to the Vector Institute . I am also the recipient of the DiDi scholarship for Biometrics research. My research interests lie at the intersection of signal processing and machine learning implemented in application for health care and biometrics. I am broadly interested in deep learning, generative models and computer vision. I graduated with a B.Sc./M.Sc, both in Electrical and Computer Engineering.

Before that, I have worked as a research fellow in the Visual Signal Analysis and Processing Center, KU, and Robotics Institute, KU and as an intern at ICUBE Laboratory at the University of Strasbourg.
